# Fairfield University Hackathon Prep

Workshop for liberal arts students preparing for a hackathon. Students bring strong domain knowledge (writing, research, analysis, creativity) but likely less technical background. Best projects lean into what they already know.

## Project Ideas by Category

### Research & Writing
- **Source Annotator** — paste an article or essay, highlight sections, add annotations and tags, export as a study guide
- **Argument Mapper** — visually map out a thesis, supporting points, and evidence as a connected node diagram
- **Citation Organizer** — paste sources, categorize them by theme, generate formatted citations

### Teaching & Communication
- **Lesson Planner** — build a class session with objectives, activities, timing blocks, and materials list
- **Discussion Board** — post topics, vote on them, organize by theme — for seminar-style classes
- **Flashcard Studio** — create, organize, and study flashcards by subject with spaced repetition

### Creative
- **Mood Board Builder** — drag-and-drop images, colors, text snippets, and fonts into a visual collage
- **Story Timeline** — plot events on an interactive timeline for a historical period, novel, or personal narrative
- **Portfolio Showcase** — not a dev portfolio, but a creative one — writing samples, art, photography, performances

### Practical / Campus Life
- **Event Planner** — plan a campus event with budget tracker, task list, timeline, and role assignments
- **Club Dashboard** — meeting notes, member directory, upcoming events, and shared resources
- **Study Group Finder** — directory where students post what they're studying, when, and where

## Top 3 Recommendations

1. **Personal Dashboard** — universally useful, everyone can customize it
2. **Study/Research Organizer** — connects directly to their academic work
3. **Event Planner** — practical group tool with obvious hackathon potential

## Notes
- All projects should be buildable as a single HTML file in a workshop session
- Each should have clear growth paths for hackathon teams to extend
- Could replace or supplement the default group project options
